# OPay Business Brain 🧠

An AI-powered business intelligence copilot for informal Nigerian micro-enterprises — built for the **OPay National Innovation Challenge 2026** by **Team NovaMind**.

## Overview

Most informal businesses in Nigeria — market traders, small shop owners, food vendors — operate without access to financial insight tools. OPay Business Brain bridges that gap with an AI copilot that understands local languages, predicts inventory needs, and helps merchants become loan-ready.

---

## The Problem

Micro-enterprises drive a huge share of Nigeria's economy, but most operate informally with no structured financial records, no early warning for stock shortages, and limited access to credit due to lack of verifiable business data.

---

## Key Features

- 🎙️ **Multilingual Voice Input** — merchants can log sales and expenses by speaking in their local language (Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo)
- 📦 **Predictive Inventory Alerts** — AI flags low stock before it becomes a problem, based on sales patterns
- 💳 **Loan Readiness Score** — a credit-style score built from real transaction history, helping merchants qualify for financing
- 🔒 **Merchant Privacy Shield** — protects sensitive business data while still enabling credit scoring
- 🗣️ **Hybrid Voice Validation Engine** — combines speech recognition with contextual validation for accuracy in noisy, informal environments
- ✅ **Regulatory Compliance** — designed with CBN, FCCPC, and NDPR frameworks in mind

---

## Tech Stack & Approach

- **AI/ML:** Voice recognition, predictive analytics for inventory forecasting
- **Compliance:** Built around CBN (Central Bank of Nigeria), FCCPC, and NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) requirements
- **Target Users:** Informal micro-enterprises (market traders, small retailers, food vendors)
